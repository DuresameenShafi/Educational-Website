ABOUT US

Our Belief:

“A single person with the right education can change the life of an entire family”

NED Scholars is a non-profit corporation. It operates exclusively for educational and charitable purposes within the meaning of Section 501 (c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code.

NED Scholars provides financial assistance on Need-cum-Merit basis to deserving students in order to support them in their STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Maths) education at Bachelor’s level. Not only scholarships but we also provide mentorships, internships, training, and financial support.

We call our scholarship recipients “Scholars”. Most of our scholars are the only one and first in their families to enter a university that offers STEM education. NED Scholars supports these scholars coming from the lower middle class and poor families, who face many challenges and hardships (like lack of money, resources, professionalism, self-esteem, motivation, and quality education) to get admission in STEM-related fields in major universities in their countries.
